Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 16, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Online ToolStack. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website and tools.

2. Data We Collect

Online ToolStack is designed with privacy in mind. Most of our tools process data locally in your browser, which means:

  • Files you upload are processed in your browser and are never sent to our servers
  • Text you enter in our tools is processed locally
  • Generated outputs remain on your device

We may collect the following minimal data:

  • Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site (page views, session duration, device type)
  • Cookies: We use cookies for analytics and Google AdSense
  • Contact Information: If you contact us, we collect your name and email address

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data to:

  • Improve our website and tools
  • Understand user behavior and preferences
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements

These services have their own privacy policies and may collect data as described in their respective policies.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site traffic
  • Serve personalized advertisements

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect website functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking
  • Disable cookies

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@toolstack.com
