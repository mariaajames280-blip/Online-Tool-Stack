What We Don't Collect

To be perfectly clear, here's what we explicitly DO NOT collect when you use Online ToolStack:

  • Personal Information: No names, email addresses, or contact details
  • Usage Data: No tracking of which tools you use or how often
  • File Content: Your files and data never leave your browser
  • Location Data: No IP address tracking or geolocation
  • Browser History: No monitoring of your browsing behavior
  • Device Information: No collection of device specs or identifiers

Technical Implementation

Our privacy-first approach is built into our technical architecture:

All tools are implemented using client-side JavaScript, HTML5 APIs, and Web Workers. Processing happens entirely within your browser's sandboxed environment. We use modern web technologies that ensure your data is processed locally without ever being transmitted over the network.

Third-Party Services

We maintain a strict no-third-party policy. Unlike many websites, we don't integrate with:

  • Advertising networks or tracking scripts
  • Analytics platforms that collect user data
  • Social media widgets or share buttons
  • External CDNs that might track users
  • Any services that could compromise your privacy

Policy Updates

Should we ever need to update this privacy policy, we will prominently display changes on our website and maintain our core commitment to user privacy. We believe in being transparent about how we handle your data, even when that means telling you we don't handle it at all.
