Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Online ToolStack. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website. This policy outlines our data collection and usage practices.

2. Information We Collect

The Short Answer: Almost Nothing!

Online ToolStack is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal information:

  • Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to understand website traffic and usage patterns. This includes anonymous data such as page views, browser type, and general location.
  • No Personal Data: We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any files, text, or data you process using our tools.
  • No Account Required: We don't require registration, so we don't collect names, emails, or passwords.

3. How Your Data is Processed

All Processing Happens Locally

This is the most important part of our privacy policy:

  • All tools run entirely in your web browser using JavaScript
  • Your files, images, text, and data NEVER leave your device
  • Nothing is uploaded to our servers
  • We cannot see, access, or store anything you process
  • Your data stays 100% private on your device

4. Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • Google Analytics Cookies: For anonymous traffic analysis
  • Google AdSense Cookies: For displaying relevant advertisements
  • No Tracking Cookies: We don't use cookies to track you across websites

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect website functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics (anonymous data only)
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements
  • CDN Services: For loading libraries and fonts (jsDelivr, Google Fonts)

These services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

6. Data Security

Since we don't collect or store your personal data or files, there's no risk of data breaches on our end. Your data security is in your hands - it never leaves your device.

7. Children's Privacy

Our tools are safe for all ages. We don't knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we don't collect personal data from anyone, this applies universally.

8. Your Rights

Under GDPR and CCPA, you have rights regarding your personal data. Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to:

  • Request access to
  • Request deletion of
  • Request correction of
  • Request portability of

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@toolstack.com
  • Contact Form: contact.html

Summary

TL;DR: We take your privacy seriously. Your data stays on your device. We use Google Analytics and AdSense. We don't collect, store, or transmit your personal files or data. Period.
