About Word Counter

This tool provides detailed analysis of your text including word count, character count, sentence count, paragraph count, and estimated reading time.

How it works:

  • Word Count: Counts all sequences of characters separated by spaces
  • Character Count: Counts all characters including spaces and punctuation
  • Characters (no spaces): Counts only letters, numbers, and punctuation
  • Sentence Count: Counts sentences based on punctuation (. ! ?)
  • Paragraph Count: Counts text blocks separated by line breaks
  • Reading Time: Estimates reading time based on average reading speed (200 words per minute)
